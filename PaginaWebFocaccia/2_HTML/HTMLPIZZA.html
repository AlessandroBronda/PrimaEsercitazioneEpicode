Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your tastebuds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday started!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberries Recips That Go Beyond Sauce
Ingredients:
For the focaccia
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 12/4 cups (350) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extre virgin olive olio, plus more for the pan
For the topping
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegard
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scarpe rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not
    necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
Method
1. Make the focaccia dought


In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all
the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the
dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate
the last of the flour...
